About

Welcome to TreeLance Academy's official Website. We set up this venture during the summer of 2020. I'm an NIT Durgapur graduate working in an OMC and my wife is a teacher. We have had plans of setting up something of this sort for quite sometime now, but never really got around to it. The pandemic allowed us to work on it and here we are! We hope to work on our services and come out with the best of resources for you folks! Happy Learning!

Presently, we have been putting our efforts in covering two different segments of learning : One for CBSE X and another for JEE/NEET

Instructions :

  • You'll find 20 questions in each test
  • You'll get 2 minutes per question. The test clock hits 0 after 40 minutes
  • There's an optional challenge mode which drops the timer to 1.5 minutes per question. Once activated, it cannot be stopped
  • You can mark a question for review in case if you have some doubt. The navigator on the right would reflect which questions have been marked for review
  • Once you hit finish and submit, the test ends and your marks is displayed on the screen
  • You can always quit the test and check the solutions given for each test
